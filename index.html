Amish Family

The Amish are known for their plain attire. Men wear solid colored shirts, broad-brimmed hats, and suits that signify similarity amongst one another. Amish men grow beards to symbolize manhood and marital status, as well as to promote humility.

Birdhouses

A birdhouse is often called a nesting house because it offers a safe place for birds to build their nests, protecting them from weather conditions and predators. During the winter season, they give birds a place to huddle together for warmth away from the cold air.

Little facts about the quilts.

Amish quilts have evolved over 2 centuries – some of the original patterns that are still popular are Double wedding ring, Tree of life, Dresden plate, Log cabin and Star of Bethlehem.

All Amish quilts are hand sewn and hand quilted. In Lancaster County especially Amish women from neighboring farms contribute to the making of these beautiful quilts.
